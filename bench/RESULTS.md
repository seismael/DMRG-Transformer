## Interpretation

* **Adam** operates on a dense `(in, out)` matrix with a tuned learning rate; its MSE depends on step count and hyperparameters.
* **Dense Exact** is the absolute $\mathcal{O}(N^3)$ minimum for an unconstrained linear regression.
* **TT-DMRG** replaces the dense weight with a rank-r Tensor-Train and converges to the rank-constrained optimum in a bounded number of bidirectional sweeps — no learning rate, no iteration budget.
