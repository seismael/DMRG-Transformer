## Interpretation

* **Adam** operates on a dense `(in, out)` matrix with a tuned learning rate; its MSE depends on step count and hyperparameters.
* **Dense Exact** is the absolute $\mathcal{O}(N^3)$ minimum for an unconstrained linear map — the lower bound any method can reach.
* **TT-DMRG** is rank-constrained (compressed) but exact *within its manifold*. On targets that actually live at rank `<= r` it matches Dense exactly (see Gate 3); on the BENCHMARK.md synthetic `sin(X W) + noise` target the rank-r trade-off is visible.
